About Us

Who we are

Echo Java is a family-owned, locally-operated cafe located in the heart of Downtown Vancouver. Established in 2004, Echo Java uses only fair-trade, 100% certified organic coffee beans roasted on-site to produce gourmet lattes and espressos. Currently, Echo Java is looking to expand into a second location in Vancouver. Echo Java’s business mission is to bring the best coffee beans from around the world to Vancouver and use the most innovative, environmentally-friendly methods to roast the beans and produce the best quality coffee and espresso drinks.
